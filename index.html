# Hello!

Welcome to my research homepage.

I'm a Ph.D Student in High Energy Physics of Yonsei University, South Korea, interested in:

- Cosmic rays
- Indirect/Direct detection of Dark matter 
- Particle physics models for Dark matter/BSM phenomena

Although I am interested in any kind of High energy physics topics, my interest is more oriented towards Particle physics model or explanation of phenemena.
These days, I am working on indirect detection of Dark matter using Fermi-LAT gamma-ray data and AMS-02 antiproton observations.
